Tasteless description:

Tasteless is the latest EP. The five songs were recorded live at Music Garage in May of 2022,
while vocals and extra goodies were tracked at home over the following months. In classic AMT fashion
each song is musically busy and specific, which made live tracking truly a bitch. This feels worth
the added pain however in a world where every bands edits every possible detail to a grid. That's rock n roll,
baby.
The songs cover a range of unorthodox topics, from dementia to the Killdozer. If it wasn't for our obsession with Tom Lehrer and
System of a Down, I'm sure these songs would be about love or something.
Tracks:
On The Fly
The Juice
Djentlemen
Sell My Shit
Tickled Pink
