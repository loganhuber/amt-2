Learn to play A Mammoth Task

These videos have Logan break down and show you how to play some of A Mammoth Task's material on the electric guitar. These songs sound a lot harder to play than they actually are, I promise. The videos are targeted for a more intermediate skill level, however I encourage you to try at whatever skill level you have! That's how you get better, you gotta give the daunting stuff a go.

For each of these lessons there will be a cooresponding play-through video. If any riffs trip you up, I highly recomend watching that part of the play-through and slowing it down. Good luck!
